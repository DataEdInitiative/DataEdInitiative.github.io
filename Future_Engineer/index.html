Seminar on Data Education for the Future Engineer

In honor of the PhD defense of Daphne Miedema, January 12, 2024. Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

Data Education for the Future Engineer.

On January 12, 2024, Daphne Miedema will defend her dissertation "On Learning SQL: Disentangling concepts in Data Systems Education". We have gathered a wonderful, inter-disciplinary committee for her dissertation, who will all come to Eindhoven in person for the defense. As such, we wanted to take this opportunity to discuss our thoughts on Data Education together.

So, on the morning of Jan 14, we will hold a seminar titled "Data Education for the Future Engineer", in which all five committee members will share considerations from their research fields on the topic of Data Education. For more information on the talks, go to the Program page.
